    About me

    Dr. Hernandez Reynolds is a dedicated and compassionate sports therapist with a passion for helping athletes reach their peak performance. With a deep understanding of human anatomy and biomechanics, Hernandez specializes in injury prevention, treatment, and rehabilitation tailored to individual needs.

    Education

  • Doctorate in Physical Therapy (DPT), University of California, Los Angeles
  • Certified Sports Massage Therapist, American Massage Therapy Association (AMTA)
  • Advanced Certification in Athletic Training, National Athletic Trainers' Association (NATA)

    • Experience

    Hernandez brings over a decade of experience working with athletes across various sports disciplines. Her expertise spans from working with professional athletes to weekend warriors, helping them overcome injuries and improve their athletic performance.

    Mission

    As a female sports therapist, Hernandez is committed to empowering athletes of all levels, genders, and ages to achieve their goals and live healthy, active lives. She strives to create a supportive and inclusive environment where athletes feel heard, valued, and empowered on their journey to recovery and success.
